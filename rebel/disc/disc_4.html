Dominant

Eliminate time-wasting elements.
Show your own competence.
Show independence.
Focus on solutions rather than problems.
Be clear about rules and expectations.
Refrain from repeating yourself.
Focus on the topic.

Respect their need for autonomy.
Allow them to be the ones who start and take the initiative.
Focus your discussion narrowly, avoid making generalizations.
When communicating with individuals of this sort, give the final result, be short.

Influential

Have a relaxed demeanor and be sociable.
Allow them to verbalize what they feel and think.
Keep the conversation light.
Approach them informally.
Use your sense of humor.
Provide written details.
Give recognition.

Steady

Reaffirm the importance of the individual to the organization.
Explain thoroughly how things are going to be done.
Allow them to adapt slowly to change.
Engage in a systematic overview.
Ensure a consistent and safe environment.
Show a sincere appreciation.

Conscientious

Be tactful and moderate in the expression of emotions.
Show seriousness and responsibility.
Be accurate and stay focused.
Provide clear deadlines and expectations.
Allow a precedent to guide you.
Demonstrate loyalty.
