Do
Ask kindly to the customer to repeat what he/she just told you, take notes on key words, paraphrase what you understood, that way you and the customer will know that you are in the same page. With the keywords that you were able to identify, you must ask probing questions to make sure of the customer’s request.​
Don't
Transfer the call to the queue again. Do not tell the customer that due to the accent you cannot help him. (You have many options to help the customer).
