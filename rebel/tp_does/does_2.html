TP Woman

TP Women is the program through which we celebrate the accomplishments and greatness of all Teleperformance women globally.

The mission of the program is focused on supporting diversity, inclusion and gender equality, seeking opportunities for everyone and equitable participation at each level of the organization.

TP contigo

TP Contigo, is a safe space and your information will be confidential.

The workplace sexual harassment committee aims to implement the necessary measures in the company to prevent, avoid and eradicate alleged workplace sexual harassment.

TP pride

TP Pride” is committed to create and nurture a culture where employees of diverse sexual orientation, gender identity, and gender expression can bring their whole and authentic selves to work.

With TP Pride, we take a step closer towards a more equal world, where respect and open-mindedness are never-ending.

No matter who you are, where you’re from, or how you express yourself as an individual, we at Teleperformance share, receive, and spread the love! We continue to foster a safe space and we are proud to fight against gender inequality together with our queer community.
